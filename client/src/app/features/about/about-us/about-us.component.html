About Us

The year is 2747. After centuries of trial, error, explosions, and surprisingly durable duct tape, humans have finally cracked the mysteries of bending time, space, and "other scientific stuff" to make space travel as easy as catching a rideshare.

Fly to Space™ is a proud subsidiary of Space-Z — not to be confused with Space-X, or the extremely unsuccessful Space-Y. Founded in 2665 by legendary explorer and business mogul Jetson Chambers, Space-Z changed everything when its team of quantum theorists accidentally invented the graviton phase-thrust loop in 2673 while trying to make coffee in zero-G.

Our proudest moment came in 2689 when the first human to reach Neptune, Zorblox Thundermint VII, planted the Fly to Space™ flag on its glistening ice plains. Rumors that he forgot to bring snacks remain unconfirmed.

Today, our company is worth $7 trillion—give or take a few asteroid mining stocks—and has safely transported more than one (1) customer. That number has gone up, we promise.
